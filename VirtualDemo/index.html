A Quick Overview

A World of Plants is about connecting people, computers and agriculture.

Analyze

View real-time readings of your plants' conditions such as temperature, humidity and moisture

Build

Build your own Plant Boxes with personalised hardware and sensors and easily add them to your profile for tracking

Control

Control plant conditions such as light colour/intensity, fan speed and water levels straight from the Web
